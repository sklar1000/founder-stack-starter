# Inbox

Dump anything here the moment it happens — a thought at 2am, a link, a quote from a call,
a feature idea, a name to follow up with. **Capture first. Sort later.**

The one rule: don't process in real time. Capturing and organizing are different jobs, and
doing them at the same time kills both. Empty this once a week (the "Friday purge"): ask
Claude *"help me process my inbox"* and it'll route each item to where it belongs —
a customer note, an assumption, a task, or the trash.
