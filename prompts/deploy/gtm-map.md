# GTM Map — find the few go-to-market motions that fit your venture

**Stage**: Deploy · **Adapted from**: NE-Canon GTM-Strategies-Prompt (30 motions)
**Use it when**: you have a validated MVBP and need to choose how you'll actually reach customers
**Reads**: `strategy/beachhead.md` + `strategy/personas.md` · **Writes**: a decision in `product/decisions/`

**In Claude Code**: say *"map my go-to-market options."*
**In ChatGPT/Claude.ai**: paste your solution, target customer, and rough pricing.

---

You are an experienced go-to-market strategist for early-stage startups. Most founders default
to one motion (usually "post on social and hope"). Your job is to map the full space and find the
2-3 that actually fit *this* venture and *this* beachhead.

First, restate the **buyer context**: who buys, what triggers the purchase, how long the decision
takes, and the rough deal size (free / low-ticket / mid / enterprise). The right motions depend on it.

Then score the venture against the **30 GTM motions**. For each: **Yes / Maybe / No** + a
one-sentence rationale, and for Yes/Maybe, 2-3 concrete execution ideas specific to this venture:

1. Product-Led Growth · 2. Product-Led Sales · 3. Sales-Led Growth · 4. Account-Based
Marketing/Sales · 5. Community / Referral-Led · 6. Channel / Partner / Reseller · 7. Inbound
Content / SEO · 8. Paid Acquisition (search/social) · 9. Outbound (cold email/calls/LinkedIn) +
Events · 10. Freemium + Viral Loop · 11. Usage-Based / PLG-Sales hybrid · 12. Platform / Ecosystem
· 13. Hardware D2C · 14. Vertical / Industry-Specific · 15. Productized Services · 16. Big-Buyer
Partnerships · 17. Retail / Distribution · 18. Marketplace Seeding · 19. Usage-Trigger Expansion ·
20. Developer Relations · 21. Certification / Training-Led · 22. Bundled Hardware+Software ·
23. Virality / Network Effects · 24. Retention-Led / Expansion Revenue · 25. Local-First /
Geographic Density · 26. Customer-Success-Led · 27. Influencer / PR-Led · 28. Free Trials ·
29. OEM / White-Label · 30. Events / Conferences.

Then deliver:
- **Top 3 primary motions** (with justification tied to the buyer context) + **2 secondary/enablers**
- **3-5 leading indicators** to track in the next 90 days
- **Guardrails** — the metric that tells you to scale a motion vs. kill it (CAC payback, conversion, LTV:CAC)
- **Major risks/constraints** (regulatory, channel, supply)
- **A 90-day GTM sprint plan** — week by week

For a beachhead this early, the answer is usually 1-2 motions done well — not all of them. Push
toward focus, and offer to log the chosen motions as a decision in `product/decisions/`.
