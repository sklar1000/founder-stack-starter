# Competition & Differentiation

Who else solves this job — and why you're meaningfully better, not marginally better.

> **New Enterprises method:** Map competitors through the **job-to-be-done lens**, not the
> category lens. Your real competition is whatever the customer hires *today* — which is
> often a spreadsheet, a manual process, or "they just live with it," not another startup.
> And the bar is **10x better, not 10% better** — switching costs are real, so "a bit nicer"
> loses.
