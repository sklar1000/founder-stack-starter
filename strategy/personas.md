# Persona

The single human inside your beachhead. Not a demographic — a specific, named person you
could picture. When you say "the customer," everyone on the team should see the same face.

> **New Enterprises method:** Build the persona from your *actual interviews*, not your
> imagination. If you can't fill this in from real conversations, you haven't done enough
> discovery yet — go talk to more people.
