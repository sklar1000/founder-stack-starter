# Market Segments

Before you pick *who* you serve, you list the candidates. A "market" is never one thing —
it's a dozen distinct groups with different pains, budgets, and reachability.

> **New Enterprises method (Market Segmentation):** Brainstorm 6+ specific segments. Get
> concrete — not "small businesses" but "solo Shopify sellers doing $5-50K/mo who run ads
> themselves." Then score each. You'll pick ONE to start (your beachhead) — but you can't
> pick well from a list of one.
