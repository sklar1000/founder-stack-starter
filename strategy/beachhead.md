# Beachhead

The ONE segment you'll dominate first. Not the biggest market — the smallest one you can
*completely own*, then expand from.

> **New Enterprises method (Beachhead):** Pick a segment so narrow it feels like a hobby.
> Win it completely. Use those happy customers + references to roll into the next adjacent
> segment (the "bowling pin" model). Facebook started at Harvard. Discord started with one
> game. Embarrassingly small is correct.
>
> **Goldilocks test:** big enough to matter, small enough to win. The customers should know
> each other (word of mouth), share the same pain, and be reachable through one channel.
