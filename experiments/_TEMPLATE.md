# Experiment — [short name]

Copy to `experiments/NNN-short-name.md` (e.g. `001-landing-page-test.md`). An experiment is
an **action that produces evidence** — it's how a risky assumption gets resolved. This is the
bridge between "what we're doing" and "what we believe."

> **New Enterprises method (test before you build):** The cheapest experiment that could
> prove you wrong beats the most beautiful product. A landing page, a fake "Buy" button, a
> manual concierge MVP, 10 cold DMs — these test demand without building the thing.
> Alberto Savoia calls it pretotyping: make sure it's **The Right It** before you build it right.
