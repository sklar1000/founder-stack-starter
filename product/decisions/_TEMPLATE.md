# Decision — [short title]

Copy to `product/decisions/YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md`. A lightweight ADR (Architecture
Decision Record — you know the format from engineering). Use it for *any* real decision:
a pivot, a pricing call, who to build for, a tech choice.

The point: **stop relitigating settled decisions.** Six weeks from now, someone will ask
"wait, why did we decide X?" This is the answer — including what you knew and gave up.

---

- **Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- **Decision**: [what you decided, in one sentence]

## Context

[What was going on. What forced the decision. What did the evidence say — link the
interviews or experiments that informed it.]

## What we traded off

[The real alternative you chose against, and what you gave up by not picking it. If there
was no trade-off, you're not making a real decision.]

## Review when

[The condition that should make you reopen this — e.g. "if we get 5 interviews contradicting
it" or "after the pilot." Not "someday."]

deciding on hope? What would change my mind?"*
